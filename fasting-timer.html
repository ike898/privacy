Privacy Policy — FastingTimer — Fasting Tracker

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. Data Collection

FastingTimer ("the App") is designed with privacy first. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All your data (fasting records, presets, and timer history) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it unless you explicitly choose to export or share.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play / Apple App Store — for in-app purchases. Purchase data is handled by the respective platform.

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads OFF

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Ad content is set to general audiences.

4. Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored in your device's local storage. We do not operate any servers or databases. If you uninstall the App, all local data is permanently deleted.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since we do not collect personal data on any server, there is no data to access, correct, or delete on our end. All data is under your control on your device.

  • Right to deletion: Uninstall the app to delete all data.
  • Right to portability: Use the app's export feature to download your data.
  • Right to opt-out: Disable personalized ads in device settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue at github.com/ike898/privacy.
